Zhihua JIN

Zhihua JIN is currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Huamin Qu. He is a member of VisLab at HKUST. Before that, he obtained his B.Eng. degree from Zhejiang University majoring in Computer Science and Technology in 2019 and was a member of Mixed Honors Class, Chu Kochen Colleges, Zhejiang University. He was supervised by Prof. Jiajun Bu during his undergraduate studies. Now his research interests mainly lie in the intersection between Machine Learning and Visualization.
